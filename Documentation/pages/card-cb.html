CARDS
Cards groups and displays information related to a product, category or service using components like text, images, badges, etc to grab the user's attention.
Oxygen provides a range of cards namely: Card with Badges | Product Card with Badge | Cards with Dismiss | Cards with Text Overlay | Text only Card | Horizontal Card
To use these cards in your project import the below stylesheet.

@import url("https://oxygen-ui-component-library.netlify.app/components/card/card.css")
